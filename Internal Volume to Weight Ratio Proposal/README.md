## Internal Volume to Weight Ratio Proposal
Proposal for independent research performed in Discovery Bay, Jamaica. Outlined is proposed testing methods and introduction as significance of research. Proposal was delivered to a leading professor for acceptance or rejection to then be performed by the author during time in Jamaica.

## Motivation
Understanding the biological decision-making processes of hermit crabs. Seeing whether a lighter and less costly shell would allow for better sexual fitness or larger shell for better protection against predators was preferred.

## Features
Paper includes background and materials and methods. Background as to why is is important to understand this vein of biology and methods to understand what materials were necessary for the trip.
